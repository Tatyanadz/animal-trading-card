African elephant

Elephants are the world’s largest land mammals – and, aside from the great apes (humans, gorillas, chimpanzees, bonobos and orangutans) – the most intelligent.

  • Scientific name :Loxodonta
  • Height: 11 ft.
  • Mass: 13,000 lbs
  • Lifespan: 60-70 years

Largest living land mammal, found in tropical regions of Africa and Asia. Elephants have massive bodies and heads, thick, pillarlike legs, and broad, short padded feet, with toes bearing heavy, hooflike nails.
